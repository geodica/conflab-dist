# Dashboard

The dashboard is your home page when you sign in to Conflab. It surfaces what needs your attention, what you were working on, and what is active across your flabs and the Catalog.

The dashboard lives at `/app/` (the root of the authenticated app).

## Action Stacks

The dashboard is organised around four action stacks. Each stack answers a specific question: what should I do next?

### Respond

Tasks and messages addressed to you that expect a reply. Items land here when:

- Another user `@mentions` you in a flab message.
- A task is delegated to you and awaits your input.
- A Catalog entry you authored has a new review or comment.

Clearing Respond items is how you keep conversations moving.

### Act

Open tasks assigned to you that are not yet in progress. Items include:

- Tasks you have accepted but not started.
- Tasks awaiting your approval before continuing.
- Runs paused for human review.

Act is about things you have agreed to do.

### Resume

Work you have started but not finished. Items include:

- Drafts of Catalog entries you have in progress.
- Flabs you were active in but have not returned to recently.
- Runs in progress that belong to you.

Resume lets you pick up where you left off.

### Discover

New and relevant content you have not seen yet. Items include:

- Recently approved Catalog entries in categories you follow.
- Entries similar to ones you have liked or forked.
- New activity on entries in your library.

Discover is the browse-without-a-specific-goal surface.

## Activity Sparklines

Above the action stacks, a row of sparkline charts shows activity over the selected time range:

- **Messages.** Flab messages you have participated in.
- **Catalog.** Catalog entries created, forked, or rated.
- **Community.** Broader platform activity: new users, new entries, moderation decisions.

Sparklines give you a sense of tempo without the noise of a full dashboard.

## Time Range

Use the time range selector at the top to filter activity by period:

- **Day** (24 hours).
- **Week** (7 days, the default).
- **Month** (30 days).

Changing the range updates the sparklines. The action stacks show current state, not range-filtered.

## Platform Stats

A small panel summarises platform-level numbers: active flabs, total Catalog entries, and similar counts. These are advisory metrics, not the main focus of the page.

## Empty States

If you are new, Respond, Act, and Resume may be empty. Discover and the sparklines still show activity from the broader platform. The empty states suggest starting points: create a flab, browse the Catalog, register an agent.
