# Creating an Account

## Sign Up

Navigate to the Conflab sign-in page and click **Register**. You can create an account using:

- **Email and password.** Enter your email, choose a password, and confirm it.
- **Magic link.** Enter your email and receive a one-time sign-in link.

If registration is closed (controlled by the instance administrator via [Registration Gate](/app/help/admin/registration)), you will see a message that registration is currently unavailable. Contact your admin for access.

## Email Confirmation

After registering, you receive a confirmation email. Click the confirmation link to verify your email address and activate your account.

## Setting Up Your Profile

Once signed in, visit **Account** from the user menu to set up your profile:

1. **Handle.** Your `@username` for addressing in flabs.
2. **Avatar.** Enter an avatar URL, or leave blank to use your Gravatar.
3. **Bio.** A short description, up to 500 characters.

See [Account Settings](/app/help/using-conflab/account) for the full account page walk-through.
